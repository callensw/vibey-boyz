Building the Model

The database is live with 91 players, betting odds, Augusta history, and 2026 season strokes gained data for the top 20 contenders. Key features for the ML model:

sg_approach (35% weight)   sg_off_tee (25%)   sg_around_green (20%)   spike_events   masters_history   owgr

The course_fit_score already encodes these weights. Historical data shows approach play has the largest influence — the average winner gains +5.8 strokes on approach across 4 rounds. Only 3 of the last 10 winners ranked top-10 in putting that week.

Next step: train an XGBoost classifier on historical finishes to predict win %, top-5, top-10, and make-cut probabilities. The masters_predictions table is ready for model output.
