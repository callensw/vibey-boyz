The Problem

Every smartphone you've ever owned was a surveillance device with a phone app attached.

iOS and Android weren't built to protect you. They were built to extract from you — your location, your attention, your behavior, your money. The hardware is designed to be opaque. The ecosystem is designed to be inescapable.

$0
What you pay for the surveillance.
What it costs you is everything else.
2B+
Devices reporting your behavior to platforms that profit from it — daily.
0
Mainstream phones with genuine hardware privacy switches. Until now.
The Shift

The app era is ending.

In 5–10 years, a fluid AI interface replaces the app grid entirely. You won't tap icons. You'll just ask. The question is who owns that interface — and where your data lives when it learns everything about you.

Apple and Google cannot build a private AI phone. Their entire business model requires the opposite. This is not a gap they can close. It is a structural impossibility.

Imagine a phone that knows you deeply — your habits, your contacts, your calendar, your voice — and that knowledge never leaves the device. No cloud. No telemetry. No one watching. Just a device that is genuinely, completely, irreversibly yours.

The Product

What makes SOVEREIGN different

🔴

Hardware Kill Switches

Physical circuit breaks for mic, camera, radios, and GPS. Not software toggles. Real switches. Nothing can override them.

🧠

AI That Runs Locally

A compact language model lives on the device. It is the interface. No queries leave your phone. No cloud knows your business.

🔓

Fully Open Stack

Open hardware schematics, open firmware, open OS. Every layer auditable by anyone. No black boxes — at any level.

💬

Conversation as Interface

No home screen. No app grid. Just ask. The AI routes your intent to the right function — contacts, calendar, maps, messages.

📵

Zero Telemetry. Ever.

No analytics. No crash reports phoning home. No behavioral tracking. The device has no commercial interest in your data.

🛡️

Call Intelligence

The AI answers unknown calls, handles spam with legal warnings, transcribes voicemails. All processed locally. None of it logged externally.

Why Now

The window just opened

2020–23

Privacy demand goes mainstream

Post-Cambridge Analytica, post-App Tracking Transparency backlash — consumers understand surveillance capitalism now. GrapheneOS hit 50K+ installs. Signal grew 400%.

2023–25

On-device AI becomes real

7B parameter models running at 30+ tokens/sec on consumer hardware. Apple Silicon and Snapdragon NPUs prove local inference isn't just possible — it's fast. The technology is credible in 2026 in a way it wasn't two years ago.

2025–26

The app era visibly ends

App store downloads plateau. AI assistants eat use cases one by one. The interface paradigm is shifting — but the platforms running it are the same ones that need your data to survive.

Now

First-mover advantage is real and brief

The open hardware movement (Fairphone, Framework, Purism) has demonstrated the supply chain is accessible. ODM partners in Taiwan and Shenzhen are ready. The manufacturing path exists. Someone builds this in the next 24 months.
