Jim Schlossnagle, who built A&M into a powerhouse before leaving for Texas, returns to Olsen Field at Blue Bell Park as the enemy. Texas is the better team on paper, but this series is a coin-flip — and the knight is going with the home crowd and the Schlossnagle curse.
