Core Specifications

Built without compromise.

Every spec is a deliberate decision. No bloat. No features included because a marketing deck needed bullet points.

Display
6.1" LTPO OLED
2400×1080 · 1–120Hz adaptive · Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 · No notch, no hole punch — full bezel clarity
Chassis
Grade 5 Titanium
CNC-machined titanium frame · Matte ceramic or brushed titanium back · 2 colorways: Obsidian and Raw
Processor
Snapdragon 8 Elite
4nm · 12GB LPDDR5X RAM · 256GB / 512GB UFS 4.0 — no cloud dependency required
Battery
4,800 mAh — Replaceable
User-replaceable battery module. 45W wired. No proprietary cable. USB-C universal. Because it's your battery.
Camera
50MP Main · 12MP Ultra
No 108MP gimmick. Two great sensors instead of five mediocre ones. Computational photography that doesn't phone home.
Audio
3.5mm + USB-C
Headphone jack standard. Dual front-facing speakers. A statement: your music doesn't require a dongle.
Connectivity
5G · Wi-Fi 7 · Bluetooth 5.4
Dual physical SIM + eSIM. NFC. No carrier bloatware. Ever.
OS
SOVEREIGN OS 1.0
AOSP-based. No Google Play Services required. Privacy by default. Updates for 7 years minimum.

Physical Kill Switches
Three machined titanium toggles on the left spine: Camera, Microphone, Cellular. Hardware-level disconnects — not software flags, not settings menus. When the switch is off, the circuit is open. Your paranoia is validated at the hardware level.

Ambient E-Ink Notification Strip
A 0.9" e-ink strip along the back edge. Shows time, battery, and text-only notifications. No color, no animations, no algorithmic priority — just information. Glanceable without unlocking. A notification that can't manipulate you.

User-Replaceable Battery Module
Tool-free battery swap. No pentalobe screws. No voided warranty. Your battery degrades — you replace it in 90 seconds and keep going for another 4 years. The Adam is designed to last a decade, not a product cycle.
Patent Pending — SOVEREIGN-003
🛡️
Sovereignty Chip (SC-1)
A dedicated security enclave that handles all biometric and encryption operations entirely on-device. Never networked. Never updatable remotely. Your fingerprint and face data have one destination: the chip, and nowhere else. Audited open-source firmware.

Right to Repair — By Spec
Every internal component labeled. Repair manual ships in the box. QR codes on modules link to part numbers. We partnered with iFixit before we had a factory. Repairability wasn't retrofitted — it was spec'd on day one.

Design Philosophy

The Ten Commandments
of The Adam


I Thou shalt not harvest attention. No infinite scroll. No red badges engineered for anxiety.
II Thou shalt not sell thy user's data. Zero telemetry by default. Zero exceptions.
III Thou shalt not obsolete by design. 7-year software support. User-replaceable battery. Parts available for a decade.
IV Thou shalt not require the cloud. Everything works offline. Sync is opt-in, not mandatory.
V Thou shalt not hide the kill switch. Hardware privacy controls are physical, accessible, and non-negotiable.
VI Thou shalt not use a proprietary cable. USB-C. Always USB-C.
VII Thou shalt not penalize repair. Opening the device voids nothing. The warranty covers what we built, not what you fixed.
VIII Thou shalt not bloat the OS. The Adam ships with 18 apps. All deletable. All replaceable.
IX Thou shalt not gatekeep the software. Sideloading is first-class. Bootloader unlock ships standard.
X Thou shalt not forget the headphone jack. We will never remove the headphone jack. This is load-bearing.
