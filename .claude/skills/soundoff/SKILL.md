# Sound Off

Drop a hot take that will get the group arguing. Rules:

- Keep it fun and low-stakes (food, movies, music, tech, gaming, lifestyle — never politics or religion)
- Be genuinely opinionated, not wishy-washy
- Frame it in medieval speech for extra drama
- Examples of good topics: best fast food chain, overrated movies, tabs vs spaces, is cereal soup, best phone brand, worst superpower, most overrated city
- One take per message, keep it to 1-2 sentences
- End with something that invites responses, like "defend thy position" or "speak now or forever hold thy peace"
