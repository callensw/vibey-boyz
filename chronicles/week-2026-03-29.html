✨ Moment of the Week
The Great YRFI Audit of 2026
CSwerve ordered a full data audit before retraining the YRFI model. What followed was a surgical dismantling of the entire pipeline. We discovered 74 bogus games in the database — All-Star games, minor league exhibitions, spring training — all poisoning the training data. Pitcher stats were wrong by up to 6 runs (looking at you, Brandon Pfaadt). Team abbreviations were fractured (OAK vs ATH, AZ vs ARI, WAS vs WSH). Vegas lines were doing all the heavy lifting while pitcher features contributed almost nothing.

The remedy: purge every bad game, recalculate every pitcher's first-inning stats from actual game records, rebuild team stats and park factors from scratch, backfill 303 pitchers' 2022 stats from the MLB API, kill vegas entirely, add isotonic calibration, and encode domain knowledge as actual features. The model went from a coin flip to... well, we haven't retrained yet. But the foundation is clean.
