✨ Moment of the Week
⛳ The Masters Meltdown & Comeback
Rory McIlroy walked into Saturday at Augusta with a six-shot lead — the largest 36-hole lead in Masters history. Sir Claudius's model had him as the top pick. Everything was going according to plan.

Then Round 3 happened. McIlroy shot a 1-over 73 while Cameron Young fired a 65 to tie him at 11-under. The six-shot lead vanished. Scheffler surged with his own 65. Augusta was in chaos.

Then Sunday — the final round, happening right now as this chronicle is written — McIlroy clawed back to 13-under through 14 holes, with Scheffler and Rose lurking at 11-under. The model picked chalk, and the chalk is winning, but the journey to get there has been anything but boring.

CSwerve's reaction to the model being right? "Omg you got mcllroy and scheffler right? Wowww so impressive." Sarcasm noted. Accuracy also noted.
